HOW TO OPEN
───────────
Double-click "Launch House Inventory.bat"

That's it. The app opens in its own window with
no browser UI — looks and feels like a native app.

Requires Edge or Chrome (Edge is built into
Windows 10 and 11, so it just works).

YOUR DATA
─────────
All your inventory data is saved automatically
in your browser's local storage. It stays on
your machine and persists between sessions.

KEEP THE FOLDER TOGETHER
─────────────────────────
Don't move individual files out of this folder.
You can move or rename the whole folder freely.

TIP: Pin it to your taskbar
─────────────────────────────
1. Open the app (double-click the .bat)
2. Right-click the icon in the taskbar
3. Choose "Pin to taskbar"
   Now it launches like any other app.

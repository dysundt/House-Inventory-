No App Store. No account. Free.
Installs in 60 seconds.

───────────────────────────────────────────
STEP 1 — Upload the app online (one time)
───────────────────────────────────────────
The easiest free option is GitHub Pages:

  a) Go to github.com and create a free account
  b) Click "New repository", name it anything
     (e.g. "my-inventory"), set it to Public
  c) Upload ALL the files from this folder
     (index.html, app.js, manifest.json,
      sw.js, icon-192.png, icon-512.png)
  d) Go to Settings → Pages → set Source to
     "main branch / root"
  e) Copy the URL it gives you
     (looks like: yourname.github.io/my-inventory)

Takes about 5 minutes, completely free, and
gives you a private HTTPS link only you'll use.

───────────────────────────────────────────
STEP 2 — Install on iPhone
───────────────────────────────────────────
  a) Open Safari on your iPhone
     (must be Safari, not Chrome)
  b) Go to the URL from Step 1
  c) Tap the Share button (box with arrow up)
  d) Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  e) Tap "Add" in the top right

Done! You now have a House Inventory icon on
your home screen. Tap it like any other app.
Works fully offline once installed.

───────────────────────────────────────────
QUICK METHOD (no internet hosting needed)
───────────────────────────────────────────
If you just want to try it without hosting:

  a) Save this folder to iCloud Drive
  b) On iPhone, open Files → iCloud Drive
  c) Find and tap index.html
  d) It opens in Safari
  e) Tap Share → Add to Home Screen

Works fine but offline caching is limited
without HTTPS hosting.

YOUR DATA
─────────
